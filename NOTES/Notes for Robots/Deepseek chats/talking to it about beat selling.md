- **Basic rights**: Allow digital distribution, limited performances, and one music video
    
- **Price point**: Set at **$XX-XXX** range based on your quality and market positioning
    
- **Clear restrictions**: No radio play, no sync licensing, no profitable live performances

### 2.2 Exclusive License Terms

**Exclusive licenses** transfer complete rights to a single artist, making the beat permanently theirs alone. This is your **premium offering** for serious artists with larger budgets. Your exclusive license should include:

- **Full ownership transfer**: The artist becomes sole owner of the beat
    
- **Unlimited usage**: No caps on streams, sales, or distributions
    
- **All rights included**: Radio, sync, performances, and merchandising
    
- **Price point**: Set at **$XXX-XXXX** range based on your reputation and beat quality
    
- **Previous license buyout**: Commitment to repurchase any existing non-exclusive licenses
    

Exclusive licenses provide **significant income spikes** and establish your value in the marketplace. [](https://create.routenote.com/blog/exclusive-vs-non-exclusive-beat-licences/)[](https://www.tunecore.com/en-in/guides/beat-licensing-101)
